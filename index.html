My name is Mathew Murray,

I'm a student at Thinkful, studying software engineering. I'm a Pennsylvania native currently living in Florida. My experience ranges from Customer Service at a small town restaurant, to manufacturing Harley's and circuit boards for Collins aerospace and SMTC in Melbourne, FL.

As a nerd, my whole life I've sought to take science fiction to reality.

Have a look around and feel free to contact me!

About me:

Growing up, people always told me "you're different". And they are right. I was diagnosed with Aspergers syndrome when I was 12. This is a form of higher function autism. This means I become super focused on projects and activities I enjoy. It was a few years later that I was introduced to website design and coding. I knew then, That was my future. I'm enrolled in Thinkful.com's engineering immersion program to apply that hyper focus and personal drive into something I can be proud of.

If you're looking for a fresh set of eyes or even a team member to collaborate with, please contact me. I'm always up for a game of Magic as well!
